\chapter{Conclusion}
This project presents a practical application of Artificial Intelligence in healthcare, aimed at supporting individuals with cognitive impairments. The system enhances patient safety, memory assistance, and independence through real-time location tracking, AI-based face recognition.
It also includes intelligent reminder modules for daily routines and important events. By automating monitoring and assistance tasks, the system improves patient independence while significantly reducing caregiver workload, forming a strong foundation for future cognitive assistive technologies.
